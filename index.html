QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in cannabis, CPG (food, beverages, cosmetics), lifestyle brands, and ecommerce. We have 12+ years of experience navigating complex regulatory environments, manufacturing constraints, and retail requirements across all these industries.

How long does a typical project take?

Standard projects ship in 4-8 weeks. This includes discovery, design, revisions, tech pack creation, and production coordination. Timelines depend on complexity, your feedback speed, and manufacturing lead times.

Can you deliver production-ready files on first submission?

Yes. We design with manufacturing constraints built in from day one. Our 0% rejection rate means we deliver files that factories accept on first submission—no reengineering required.

What about cannabis compliance?

We stay current with cannabis regulations across all major states. We handle child-resistant closures, warning labels, tracking codes, and packaging material requirements specific to each state's regulations.

Do you provide tech packs and manufacturing specs?

Absolutely. Every project includes detailed technical specification documents with measurements, materials, print specifications, tolerances, and manufacturing instructions—ensuring zero ambiguity with factories.

Can you build ecommerce sites?

Yes. We build high-converting ecommerce sites from the ground up. We handle design, development, SEO, payment processing, and optimization to drive revenue—not just traffic.

What's the pricing structure?

Projects start at $4,000 per SKU and scale based on complexity. We respond within 24 hours to inquiries and provide detailed quotes after our discovery call. We work with startups to Fortune 500 brands.
