Questions

FAQ

What industries do you specialize in?+

Cannabis, CPG/wellness, fashion/apparel, and entertainment. We've designed compliant packaging for 80+ brands across these sectors — from child-resistant cannabis to FDA-ready supplements.

How long does a typical project take?+

4-8 weeks from discovery to production-ready files. Complex multi-SKU projects may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the strategy call.

What does "production-ready" mean?+

Every file we deliver is ready to go directly to the printer, factory, or deployment server — no rework needed. Packaging includes die-lines, bleed, and color profiles. Apparel includes full tech packs with grading. Web projects launch live.

Do you handle cannabis compliance?+

Yes — multi-state regulatory compliance is core to what we do. Child-resistant packaging, warning labels, THC/CBD accuracy, and state-specific requirements. Zero compliance rejections in 8+ years.

What's included in a tech pack?+

Flat sketches, construction details, fabric/material specs, colorways with Pantone references, size grading, fit comment sheets, trim details, and label placement. Factories accept our packs on first submission.

Can you build our ecommerce site too?+

Absolutely. We build full-stack ecommerce — product pages, checkout, inventory, email flows. SkynPatch saw 342% revenue growth after our site + packaging redesign. We also build operational dashboards and automation systems.

What are your rates?+

Projects start at $4,000/SKU for packaging, $3,500 for tech packs, and $8,000+ for ecommerce builds. We scope every project individually during the strategy call — no surprises, no hidden fees.
